Our Story

Crafted by Hand, Poured with Intention

Every Aura & Ash candle begins in a small Vermont atelier, where we hand-pour each vessel in batches of no more than two dozen. Nothing is rushed, and nothing is automated.

We work only with 100% natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and phthalate-free fragrance oils — because a true ritual shouldn't come with compromises.
